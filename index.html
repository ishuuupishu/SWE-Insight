Why Pursue Software Engineering?

The BSc in Software Engineering is a comprehensive 48-month undergraduate program designed for aspiring technologists seeking a Bachelor of Science degree from our esteemed department. With a curriculum spanning 148 credits of theory and lab courses, complemented by a 12-credit internship at a reputable software development firm, students accumulate a total of 160 credits to graduate. This outcome-based program integrates foundational science and mathematics courses, general education modules, core computer science subjects, and specialized studies in software analysis, design, development, and quality assurance. Emphasizing the latest industry trends, our program instills in graduates a commitment to continuous learning and skill enhancement, fostering a pursuit of excellence essential for thriving in the dynamic field of software engineering.
